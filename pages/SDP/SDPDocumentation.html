4.1 SDP Logic

4.1.1 SDP Hierarchy

There are various levels within the SDP data for categorising the data including; swimlanes, tasks, activities and milestones.

Level Count
Swimlanes Count 13
Tasks Count 155
Activities Count 729
Special Activities Count 53
Milestones Count 7
4.1.1.1 Swimlanes

Swimlanes are the highest hierarchical level in SDP and cover specific aspects of the product development plan.

There are currently 12 swimlanes in SDP, including the Milestones swimlane (ignoring the special “-” swimlane used for special data types).
